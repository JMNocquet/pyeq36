PYEQ: full time-dependent slip inversion

PYEQ allows full time-dependent slip inversion from GNSS time series.

Approach

PYEQ models increment slip so that, at a given date, the sum of past increment slip multiplied by the Green functions equals the displacement given by the time series.

Main features

  • variable model time step

  • variable regularization constraints
